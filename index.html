We Are Atelier Bubble Tea

A female led gaming studio consisting of Amy and Kim. We love creative pursuits, video games and good food.

Our Games


Rainy Days - A slice of life visual novel about autumn coats, fat cats and the smell of coffee. Emi Simms comes home to her suburban town life after college with her energy low and her expectations about her prospects even lower? Will the autumn rain wash away her malaise or make it worse? The debut work of Atelier Bubble Tea.

Hellflowers - Maxwell had a thing for numbers and sleeping in. One day, a handsome gentleman with a crimson flower pinned to his chest comes to him with a proposition. Join his cohort and leave this mundane world behind. A psychological visual novel about numbers, flowers and a thousand year old mystery.
